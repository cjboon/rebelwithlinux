CODEMUD

The Programmer's Dungeon

Welcome, brave programmer! You've entered a dungeon where bugs are monsters and code is your weapon. Use your programming knowledge to survive!

🎮 How to Play

  • Explore the dungeon using north, south, east, west
  • Use programming commands to cast spells and attack monsters
  • Defeat monsters to gain XP and gold
  • Level up to learn new commands
  • Find and equip better gear
  • Death means starting over!

⚔️ Combat Commands

  • compile [target] - Basic attack (costs 5 MP)
  • debug [target] - Powerful attack (costs 15 MP)
  • execute [target] - Ultimate attack (costs 30 MP)
  • grep [target] - Search for weakness (reveals HP)
  • git commit - Save your progress (heal 20 HP)

🛡️ Other Commands

  • look - Examine your surroundings
  • inventory or i - View inventory
  • equipment or eq - View equipment
  • get [item] - Pick up an item
  • wear [item] - Equip an item
  • remove [item] - Unequip an item
  • drink [potion] - Use a potion
  • who - View player stats
  • help - Show all commands
