// Why This Matters

Learn Linux. Own Your Systems. Reclaim Your Freedom.

Every time you use proprietary software, you're trusting corporations with your data. They're not earning that trust—they're extracting value from you while telling you what's best for your computer.

Linux isn't just an alternative—it's a philosophy. When you run Linux, you own your operating system. When you use FOSS encryption tools, only you hold the keys to your data.

This isn't about being a "hacker" or "techie." It's about digital sovereignty. It's about privacy as a human right. It's about refusing to be the product.

The revolution will not be proprietary.

// About This Site

Built from scratch as a personal project driven by curiosity and hands-on learning. Every guide, test, and interactive element reflects a commitment to practical education in Linux, DevOps, and web technologies.

// Technical Approach

The server hosting this site runs entirely from the command line on Debian—managed via SSH without control panels or managed services. Every line of code, every configuration, every deployment decision made manually. A firm believer in Free and Open Source Software, this approach prioritizes control, transparency, and understanding systems from the ground up.
